About SEP in BCA

In the context of a Bachelor of Computer Applications(BCA),SEP stands for State Education Policy.Specifically implemented across universities in Karnataka(such as Bengaluru City University, Bangalore University, and Bidar University), this Choice Based Creadit System(CBCS) outlines the curriculum, grading, and examination standards for the degree.

Key features of the SEP BCA Curriculum:

  • Duration & Semesters:The undergradduate degree spans 3 years, divided into 6 Semesters.Ecah semester consists of at least 90 working days.
  • Course Structure:
  • Semesters 1-5:Typically include core theory papers and hands-on practical/lab sessions
  • Semester 6:Usually consists of fewer theory papers, advanced practicals, and a mandatory major project/internship.
  • Electives:Students can choose specialized subject(like Internet Basic, Cyber,Security, or E-Commerce)in later semesters.
  • Evaluation & Grading
    Performance is evaluted throudh a continuous internal assessment(Formative) and a final University Exam(Summative).
  • Internal Assessment(IA):Carries 20 marks based on tests, assignments, and attendance/
  • Semester End Exam(SEE):Carries 80 marks for a duration of 3 hours.
